Hello! My name is Linda and I am a graphic designer with a love for art and illustration. I've gone through quite a journey get to where I am today but I'm the happiest where I am now as a designer being able to push forward ideas and concepts into something living and breathing. I had my start with a love for art at the age of 4 and that love catapulted me into the world of design my sophormore year in college at the Univeristy of California, Davis. Design gave me a voice and I want to be able to give others one as well.
